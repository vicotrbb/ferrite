# Code of conduct

Ferrite contributors and maintainers must make participation respectful,
welcoming, and free from harassment.

## Expected behavior

- Be constructive, specific, and kind in technical disagreement.
- Respect different experience levels, identities, and backgrounds.
- Focus criticism on code, evidence, and outcomes, not people.
- Accept correction, protect private information, and give credit.

## Unacceptable behavior

Harassment, threats, sexualized conduct, discriminatory language, personal
attacks, deliberate disruption, doxxing, and sustained bad-faith behavior are
not permitted in project spaces.

## Enforcement

Report conduct concerns privately through the repository's
[security advisory form](https://github.com/vicotrbb/ferrite/security/advisories/new)
and begin the report title with `Conduct report`. Maintainers may edit or remove
content, warn participants, restrict participation, or ban contributors when
necessary. Reports will be handled as confidentially as practical, with
conflicts of interest disclosed and avoided.
