# Research Notes

Research notes update or refine the baseline research in `../../research`.

Create files as:

`YYYY-MM-DD-short-topic.md`

## Template

```markdown
# Topic

Date: YYYY-MM-DD

## Question

What are we trying to learn?

## Current Baseline

What does existing Ferrite research or prior evidence say?

## New Evidence

What sources, experiments, codebases, papers, or benchmarks were reviewed?

## Interpretation

What does the evidence imply for Ferrite?

## Decision Impact

Does this require an ADR, implementation change, benchmark, or theory note?

## Open Questions

What remains unknown?
```
